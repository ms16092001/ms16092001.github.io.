FEOSOL

What is it?

Feosol is an iron supplement used by people concerned about the amount of iron in their blood. Iron is vital to the transport of oxygen in the bloodstream to the cells in the body. Uses

This medication is an iron supplement used to treat or prevent low blood levels of iron (such as those caused by anemia or pregnancy). Iron is an important mineral that the body needs to produce red blood cells and keep you in good health.

Side-Effects

Common side effects of Feosol include but are not limited to upset stomach, stomach cramps, constipation, diarrhea, or black or bloody stools. Allergic reactions to ferrous sulfate are extremely rare. Although iron is a naturally occurring and critical part of our diet, overdosing on iron can lead to iron poisoning and even death. Always follow your doctor's instructions or the suggested dosage on the supplement.
