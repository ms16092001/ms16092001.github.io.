About Hospital

1. Nanavati Hospital has a distinct care unit, which includes multiple cancer care programs such as medical oncology program, radiation oncology program, surgical oncology program, robotic surgery program, cytoreductive surgical program, cancer reconstructive surgery, and bone marrow transplant program.
2. The department is well-equipped with advanced infrastructure such as Halcyon (Latest linear accelerator), Sentinel Lymph node biopsy, HIPEC technology, Immunotherapy, IMRT, IGRT, digital mammography and many more.
3. The cancer unit provides nutrition services for cancer patients, physiotherapy & rehabilitation, patient & family counselling, and palliative care in cancer.

Infrastructure & Facilities

1. Hospital has 350 beds in total, the hospital has one of the biggest Critical Care Units in Western India with 75 beds dedicated to different specialties.
2. The Out-Patient Department covers more than 75,000 sq ft.
3. The heart institute is equipped with Allura Xper FD 10 System, which is first of its kind in Mumbai.
