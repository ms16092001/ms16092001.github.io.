About Hospital

1. The hospital has a dedicated center to treat disorders related to Haematology and Medical Oncology, which is fully equipped to treat ailments like breast cancer, thyroid cancer, brain cancer etc.
2. It also has an advanced diagnostic lab including PET-CT scan, 3 Tesla MRI, Flow cytometry, immunohistochemistry, and molecular diagnostic.
3. There is a unique section entirely dedicated to academics and research, which continuously strives to search for new technologies and methods for better treatment options.

Infrastructure & Facilities

1. Built over 43 acres of land, the hospital is furnished with 45 operation theatres, 1250 beds and over 350 critical care beds under 20 specialties.
2. It also provides 20 treatment beds that are functional at any given point of time.
3. It is equipped with 256 Slice Ct, Brain Suite, Intra-Operative Imaging Operating Theater, Da Vinci Robot for Minimal Invasive Surgery, Artis- Zeego Endovascular Surgical Cath Lab, 4 Linear Accelerators (provision for IGRT/ IMRT) (radiation surgery), Tomotherapy, Integrated Brachytherapy Unit with remote controlled HDR, 3.0 Tesla MRI, PET CT, Gamma Camera, Digital X-Ray, Fluoroscopy, Bone Densitometry, 3D and 4D Ultra Sound and Digital Mammography.
