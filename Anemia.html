Anemia caused by blood cells

What is it?

In this type You can lose red blood cells through bleeding. This can happen slowly over a long period of time, and you might not notice it.

CAUSES MAY INCLUDE..



1. Gastrointestinal conditions such as ulcers, hemorrhoids, gastritis (inflammation of your stomach), and cancer
2. Non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) such as aspirin or ibuprofen, which can cause ulcers and gastritis
3. A womanâ€™s period, especially if you have a heavy menstruation (or heavy period). This can be associated with fibroids.
4. Post-trauma or post-surgery as well.

Cure

Treatment for anemia usually involves taking iron supplements and making changes to your diet. If the underlying cause of iron deficiency is loss of blood - other than from menstruation - the source of the bleeding must be located and stopped. This may involve surgery.
