SLOW FE

What is it?

Slow Fe is a slow-release over-the-counter iron supplement used to treat or prevent low blood levels of iron caused by iron deficiency anemia, or pregnancy. The active ingredient in this supplement is ferrous sulfate which works by replenishing your body's supply of iron which helps to prevent symptoms such as fatigue and loss of energy. Slow Fe slow release iron supplement comes in the form of an oral tablet and is best used on an empty stomach. If you experience an upset stomach, you may take this medication with food. Follow all directions on the product packaging when taking this supplement or ask your doctor for medical advice. Avoid using dairy products, antacids, or tea before or after using this supplement as it may decrease its effectiveness. These slow-release tablets should be taken whole. Do not crush or chew them as this will cause the medication to be released all at once which may increase the risk of side effects.

Uses

This medication is an iron supplement used to treat or prevent low blood levels of iron (such as those caused by anemia or pregnancy). Iron is an important mineral that the body needs to produce red blood cells and keep you in good health.

Side Effects

Common side effects may include constipation, diarrhea, stomach cramps or upset stomach. These effects are usually temporary and may go away as your body adjusts to this medication. Contact your doctor or pharmacist if you experience any of these effects and they persist or worsen. Serious allergic reactions to this drug are rare. However, you should seek emergency medical attention if you begin to notice signs of an allergic reaction such as rash, itching, swelling, severe dizziness, or trouble breathing. Talk to your doctor or healthcare provider for more information on the possible side effects involved with using this supplement as this is not a complete list.
