INTEGRA PLUS

What is it?

Integra Plus is part of the Multivitamin and Mineral Supplements class and treats Anemia and Iron Deficiency. Multivitamin and mineral supplements are used to provide higher concentrations of vitamins and minerals for prenatal nutrition, iron deficiency, and nutritional deficiency.

Uses

Uses of Integra Plus:
1. It is used to help growth and good health.
2. It may be given to you for other reasons. Talk with the doctor.
3. It is used to treat or prevent low iron in the body.

Side-Effects

Signs of an allergic reaction, like rash; hives; itching; red, swollen, blistered, or peeling skin with or without fever; wheezing; tightness in the chest or throat; trouble breathing, swallowing, or talking; unusual hoarseness; or swelling of the mouth, face, lips, tongue, or throat. Very upset stomach or throwing up. Black, tarry, or bloody stools. Fever. Very bad belly pain. Throwing up blood or throw up that looks like coffee grounds. Stomach cramps.
